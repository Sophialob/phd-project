Help & Contact Information

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this study, or experience increasing levels of distress in the hours or days after completing the survey, please contact the main researcher responsible for this PhD project, Sophia Lobanov-Rostovsky (sophia.lobanov-rostovsky@lshtm.ac.uk) or the PhD project supervisors, Dr. Joelle Mak (joelle.mak@lshtm.ac.uk) or Prof. Loraine Bacchus (loraine.bacchus@lshtm.ac.uk).
